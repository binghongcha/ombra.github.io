Menu

We’re offering delivery and collection of a range of fresh pasta, sauces, wines, general provisions and of course, tiramisu. In addition we have grocery essentials such as eggs, tinned tomatoes and Italian ground coffee. We are also supporting Flourish Farm by existing as a collection point for their veg boxes. These are ordered directly through them but please let us know if you would like to coincide your collection with any of our offering.

When


Ombra is open from Tuesday to Sunday. Lunch is served from 12pm-3pm and dinner is served from 6pm-10pm.

We open everyday at 10.30 for coffee and pastries. From Tuesday to Friday we serve a lunch menu at £12 for a pasta and a glass of wine. We rest on Sunday night and Mondays.
